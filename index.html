RoboCup 2023 Bordeaux

This was our first time on the international RoboCup. The robot hardware was quite basic:

  • Main processing unit (object detection, logic): Raspberry Pi 4
  • Camera: Arducam B0310 (120 FOV, 12MP), directed forward
  • IMU: Adafruit BNO055
  • Secondary processing unit (line sensors, motors): Teensy 4.1
  • Motors: Pololu #3202 (1000 RPM) / Pololu #3203 (500 RPM)
  • Motor driver: VNH5019
  • Omni wheels: GTF Robots (50 mm) / self made
  • Line sensors: OSRAM SFH-4656 + Knightbright AP2012P3C

For the software, we decided to fully utilize the potential of Raspberry Pi and use multiprocessing - every information processing and interface handling runs in the separate process.
